# Warehouse Automation: A Practical Guide

Warehouses are the heart of modern logistics. As order volumes grow and labor becomes harder to find, automation is helping teams move faster without sacrificing accuracy.

## Where to Start

Not every warehouse needs robots on day one. A smart approach is to automate the most repetitive tasks first.

- **Barcode scanning and WMS integration** improve inventory accuracy.
- **Conveyor systems** reduce walking time for pickers.
- **Automated storage and retrieval systems (AS/RS)** save space and labor.

## Measuring Success

Track metrics that matter:

| Metric | Why It Matters |
| --- | --- |
| Picking accuracy | Fewer returns and complaints |
| Orders per hour | Higher throughput |
| Labor hours per order | Lower operational cost |

Start small, measure results, and scale what works.
