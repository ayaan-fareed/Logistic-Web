# Preparing for Peak Season Air Freight

Peak season can turn air freight from a routine booking into a capacity battle. Whether it is holiday retail, year-end manufacturing deadlines, or urgent project cargo, demand spikes can push rates up and split-time cargoes off schedule.

## Plan before the rush

1. **Lock capacity early**: Block-space agreements and forward allocations protect both price and lift.
2. **Build flexibility into routings**: Alternate gateway options can keep shipments moving when primary lanes clog.
3. **Pack smarter**: Dimensional weight optimisation reduces chargeable weight and total cost.

## Visibility matters most

During high-volume windows, tracking updates every few hours are not a luxury — they are a necessity. Real-time dashboards let operations teams spot delays before they cascade into downstream failures.
