# Reduced Import Delays & Storage Costs

A mid-sized importer was facing rising demurrage charges and unpredictable port delays across more than 700 containers every quarter. After reviewing their end-to-end flow, our team identified three choke points: customs document preparation, carrier booking timing, and inland transport scheduling.

## What we changed

- **Pre-clearance workflow**: We digitised documentation checks so declarations were lodged before vessels berthed.
- **Carrier window management**: Booking confirmations were tied to a rolling 7-day forecast, reducing last-minute scrambles.
- **Inland optimisation**: Empty container returns and de-hires were batched by region, cutting empty kilometres by 22%.

## Results

Within six months, average container dwell time dropped by 35%, storage costs fell by nearly 30%, and the client’s monthly landed-cost variance narrowed dramatically. The biggest win was predictability — planners could finally promise reliable arrival dates to their own customers.
