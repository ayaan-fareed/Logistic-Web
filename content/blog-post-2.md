# The Future of Last-Mile Delivery

The final leg of delivery is often the most expensive and the most important. Customers expect speed, accuracy, and transparency, putting pressure on logistics providers to innovate.

## Key Trends

1. **Electric and low-emission fleets** are reducing environmental impact.
2. **Micro-fulfilment centers** bring inventory closer to customers.
3. **Route optimization software** cuts delivery time and fuel costs.

### Customer Expectations

Same-day and next-day delivery are no longer luxuries. They are baseline expectations. Companies that can meet them reliably gain a serious advantage.

```text
Faster delivery = Higher customer retention
```

Investing in last-mile innovation is no longer optional. It is the new standard.
