# Customs Compliance Checklist for 2026

Regulatory changes in 2026 are reshaping how imports are classified, valued, and reported. Staying compliant means fewer inspections, faster clearances, and reduced exposure to penalties.

## Pre-shipment essentials

- Verify Harmonised System (HS) codes against the latest tariff schedules.
- Confirm country of origin and any applicable free-trade agreement claims.
- Prepare commercial invoices, packing lists, and certificates of origin in advance.

## At the border

- Lodge entries electronically as early as permitted.
- Respond to customs queries within the required window.
- Maintain consistent valuation methodology across related-party transactions.

## Ongoing maintenance

Review your compliance programme quarterly. Classification rulings and valuation benchmarks should be updated whenever product specifications or supplier structures change.
