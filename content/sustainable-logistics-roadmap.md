# A Roadmap to Sustainable Logistics

Sustainability in logistics is no longer only about brand image. Customers, regulators, and investors are asking for measurable emissions reductions across the supply chain.

## Start with data

Before choosing solutions, baseline your emissions by transport mode, lane, and carrier. Most improvements come from three levers: modal shift, consolidation, and route optimisation.

## Quick wins

- Combine less-than-container-load (LCL) shipments to raise container utilisation.
- Shift short-haul air freight to road or rail where time allows.
- Use warehouses closer to demand centres to shorten last-mile distances.

## Long-term moves

Sustainable fuels, electric last-mile fleets, and carbon-offset programmes can extend a roadmap, but only after the easy operational gains have been captured.
